102 Ronald Perelman Quotes

You know, people get married for a whole bunch of reasons. Myself - I was very fortunate to be with four terrific women. And I’ve got six fabulous children. And I would not change one of those marriages. I learned from each of them. I grow from each of them. Each had its own issues and problems, but I wouldn’t change one of them.
Ronald Perelman

[On whether he had a favorite amongst his four ex-wives] I wouldn’t tell you if I did.
Ronald Perelman

[On being a private man and yet being regularly in the tabloids] Tell me how to get rid of that.
Ronald Perelman

[On him seeing his name on the front page.] I never got real satisfaction out of the good ones, and I used to get really annoyed with the bad ones, so I just stopped reading them all.
Ronald Perelman

[As a child being involved in his father’s board meetings of the family businesses.] I was always fascinated by the decision-making process and the managerial process and just business in general. So every opportunity I had to hang around with him, I did.
Ronald Perelman

[On his father] He’s very good at his job. He’s a very clear thinker. He was a great believer in decision-making. Making decisions.
Ronald Perelman

[On his father and him working in the business] He let me work on my own to a far greater extent than I should’ve been - just to learn as I was making my own mistakes.
Ronald Perelman

[On leaving his father’s company] I wanted to do more on my own.
Ronald Perelman

The concept of a ‘hostile transaction’ is really a misnomer. Because you can’t buy something unless the owner wants to sell it. Now, it might be hostile to the management, who don’t want to lose their jobs or don’t want to work for somebody else, but every deal that gets done by definition is a friendly deal.
Ronald Perelman

[On the ‘Barbarians at the gate’ image.] The weak either improved or got swallowed up. I think that is good for the economy.
Ronald Perelman



[On some people being gifted at making money] I think it’s an aptitude that you have, that you intrinsically feel, just like a good violinist or a good piano player or a good soccer player. Certain people easily pick up certain things, and I think business is one of those.
Ronald Perelman

[On cigars] It’s an acquired taste. If I’m walking down the street and somebody is smoking a good cigar, my mouth begins to water.
Ronald Perelman

[More on cigars] I stopped smoking cigars six years ago, but a good one still gets my attention. And I’m still trying to figure out what to do with my hands.
Ronald Perelman

[On defining a cigar] It’s like a pacifier for a grown-up.
Ronald Perelman

[On a gifts for a woman someone want’s to impress.] I think it depends on the level of commitment that the two people have. Flowers are always loved. I don’t send them. I’m always criticized for that.
Ronald Perelman

Those who send flowers do not get criticized.
Ronald Perelman

[On whether he tapes phone conversations] Absolutely not. Not true. Nobody would ever talk to me if that was true.
Ronald Perelman

Music plays a very important role in my life.
Ronald Perelman

I’m a frustrated musician. I play the drums.
Ronald Perelman

Every corporate gig that we have, the musicians traditionally have let me play with them, so I’ve played with some very interesting people.
Ronald Perelman



I think it’s more effective if you’ve got real control of the English language - to tell the other person, in five words, what an ass you think they are. But I unfortunately don’t have that ability. The Brits do. I marvel at that.
Ronald Perelman

All I hope for is that I leave the world a better place for my children.
Ronald Perelman
