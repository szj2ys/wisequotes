The poet cries, "Backward, turn backward, oh time in your flight," but he cries in vain, for tune flows only ahead.
Napoleon Hill

The most beneficial use of time is that which one devotes to silent meditation when searching for guidance from within.
Napoleon Hill

Time spent in silent thought may yield fabulous riches through the creation of sound ideas.
Napoleon Hill

When there is no work for your hand to perform, let your mind be employed so not a second of time will be wasted.
Napoleon Hill

Every minute of time saved on any job is a step on the road to promotion.
Napoleon Hill

Napoleon Hill

Most misfortunes are the results of misused time.
Napoleon Hill

Napoleon Hill

Time, the great universal doctor, can cure all human ills, and most of them very quickly.
Napoleon Hill

Remember there is always an ending for everything except time and space.
Napoleon Hill

Napoleon Hill
