Invest your time in worthy projects; invest your thoughts toward a worthy purpose; invest your talents toward a worthy occupation; invest your affection toward a worthy recipient; and finally, reserve your greatest respect for yourself, for it is that image … what you perceive yourself to be … that determines the quality of life.
Jim Rohn

Do not doubt yourself, for where doubt resides, confidence cannot. Do not neglect yourself, for with neglect comes loss. Do not imagine yourself to be less than you are, nor more than you are, but seek always to become all of which you are capable. Do not allow yourself to become arrogant or discourteous, for both are characteristics adopted by those who seek to cover their weaknesses. Do not spend time regretting the past, but invest that time wisely by preparing a better future.
Jim Rohn

You are deserving – you are becoming – and you shall succeed.
Jim Rohn

The fall tells us if we have really done that which is required, or if we have fooled ourselves through the temporary aesthetic of conversation and pretense … of telling ourselves we’ve worked when we haven’t.
Jim Rohn

As you sow, so shall you reap.
Jim Rohn

Those who do not possess will always scorn the possessor.
Jim Rohn

Those who condemn the successful man or woman for their apparent good fortune or dishonesty are unaware of the price often paid for success. They cannot see the massive disappointments, the shattered hope, or the broken dreams. They do not understand the risks incurred in both raising and investing capital for an idea yet unproven.
Jim Rohn

The arrival of winter finds us in one of two categories: Either we are prepared or we are unprepared.
Jim Rohn

We are meant to constantly improve our conditions, ourselves, and our results. We either improve, or we regress, for never do we remain the same.
Jim Rohn

To those who are unprepared, the arrival of winter is a time for regret and a time for sorrow. Having lacked the willingness to pay the pain of earlier discipline, we now pay the heavier pain of regret.
Jim Rohn



For things or circumstances to change, human attitudes, opinions, and habits must change.
Jim Rohn

Showing a profit means touching something and leaving it better than you found it.

Take time to gather up the past so that you will be able to draw from your experiences and invest them in the future.
