Having a property manager enables you to work on your business, not in it.
Dolf de Roos

When you invest in residential property, you are essentially dealing with people… On the other hand, when you deal with commercial property, you are essentially dealing with contracts.
Dolf de Roos

Commercial tenants want to improve their (your!) property all the time, whereas residential tenants do not really care.
Dolf de Roos

No matter how much you drop the rent, no photographer looking for a studio is likely to settle for the tomato cannery.
Dolf de Roos

The nature of residential properties is such that once you get to own about twenty of them, you almost have a full-time job looking after them.
Dolf de Roos

Of all the extremely high-net-worth property investors I know, only two own predominantly residential properties. The rest all own commercial.
Dolf de Roos

I believe that property markets are the biggest and most free markets anywhere in the world.
Dolf de Roos

The reason why property works so well as an investment vehicle all over the world is largely because it is self-directed and self-regulated through the natural forces of supply and demand.
Dolf de Roos

Investing in real estate is definitely infectious. You either love it, or you don’t. If you don’t, you can’t fake it, and if you do, you can’t hide it.
Dolf de Roos

If driving around town looking at properties is a chore to you, then property is probably not for you.
Dolf de Roos



Always buy from a motivated seller.
Dolf de Roos

Never be the first to name a figure – that person always loses.
Dolf de Roos

Be countercyclical.
Dolf de Roos

Seldom sell.
Dolf de Roos

Dare to try. You either win or learn. And you never learn less.
Dolf de Roos

The deal of the decade comes along about once a week.
Dolf de Roos

I haven’t played Monopoly in decades on a board because my life has become one huge game of Monopoly. I play the game every day.
Dolf de Roos

Real estate is truly international.
Dolf de Roos

As some stage, I encourage you to stop reading and start doing!
Dolf de Roos

We are going to be dead for a mighty long time. Every moment counts. Make the most of life.
Dolf de Roos
