At Amazon, we’re very focused on invention. If you look at the things we focus on, we’re never trying to create a ‘me-too’ product offering.
Jeff Bezos

Jeff Bezos

I totally excited about what can be achieved over time… I know that it’s just the beginning.

I realized you could be a book store on the web that could hold more books than any physical bookstore could ever hold.
Jeff Bezos

Books were the first best product to sell online.
Jeff Bezos

I’ve always been a big reader… But that’s not the reason why we choose to sell books online.
Jeff Bezos

Jeff Bezos
