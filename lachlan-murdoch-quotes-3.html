Lachlan Murdoch Quotes

101 Lachlan Murdoch Quotes

We all have to avoid the perils of that narrow-mindedness that threatens to narrow our future; to restrict our opportunity - at a time when that future and opportunity is vast.
Lachlan Murdoch

No media is more worthy than any other because of the age, income or status of its target audience.
Lachlan Murdoch

There is no room for dictating taste in the diverse and dynamic world of media. To limit taste only limits the role we play for people of all kinds.
Lachlan Murdoch

The profit motive is not only fundamental to our ability to reward shareholders and pay employees; it’s fundamental to excellent journalism.
Lachlan Murdoch

As Baz Luhrmann once put it: ‘Our currency is not dollars and cents. Our currency is stories. Dollars and cents are the by-product’. A by-product that allows us to constantly improve the real currency, the story.
Lachlan Murdoch

Our profits enable us to grow as we seek to meet the increasing demands of an increasing number of readers and viewers in a challenging and fragmented marketplace.
Lachlan Murdoch

Profits have increased competition, not lessened it, and made our media landscape far richer.
Lachlan Murdoch

The ability to move individuals and intellect from all over the globe has given Australia an enormous benefit…
Lachlan Murdoch

I am amused that in Australia, News is often referred to as a US company, while in the US and in the UK we are seen culturally and legally, as very much Australian.
Lachlan Murdoch

Without media companies driving for profits Australians would be bereft of many of the advances and services they now rely on.
Lachlan Murdoch



People say Australians are the most egalitarian people in the world. I certainly believe we are.
Lachlan Murdoch

My grandfather, in his will, said that he wanted his children to ‘have the great opportunity of spending a useful, altruistic and full life in newspaper and broadcasting activities’.
Lachlan Murdoch

Media is much more than an outlet for news; it is a forum for opinions, emotions and shared convictions that strengthen us all when we need strength most.
Lachlan Murdoch

Profits fund the excellence of our media services and the high quality of our products.
Lachlan Murdoch

Great journalism needs profits, it needs to be broad minded and it needs to always steer clear of elitism.
Lachlan Murdoch

[On Channel 10] Since Renovators has come off, our ratings have bounced back very strongly.
Lachlan Murdoch

[On Channel 10] We will certainly achieve over 30% revenue share next year.
Lachlan Murdoch

I can confirm, on the record, that I have never considered or close to considered becoming a Scientologist in any way or at any time.
Lachlan Murdoch

I probably come close to sharing my father's views about the religion, but I resist tweeting them.
Lachlan Murdoch

One of the things that parents do, good parents do is that they lead by example. And by leading by example you want to emulate their work ethics and emulate their role in the community.
Lachlan Murdoch



You want to do the best you can do. Whatever you do…
Lachlan Murdoch
