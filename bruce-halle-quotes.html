People sometimes say, ‘Gee, how did you do what you do? How did you build the company?’ Well, I worked at it for 50 years…
Bruce Halle

Little by little, all the pieces kind of come together.
Bruce Halle

Be fair, be truthful, work hard, be there on time, and help people.
Bruce Halle

I think of myself as an ordinary guy who goes to work every day…
Bruce Halle

Go forth into this land and make your dreams come true.
Bruce Halle

Find something to like in everyone.
Bruce Halle

Be honest. Work hard. Have fun. Be grateful. Pay forward.
Bruce Halle

There’s nothing wrong with stealing a good idea.
Bruce Halle

It’s not because we were brilliant or smart.
Bruce Halle

We were just lucky.
Bruce Halle



It’s sometimes better to be lucky than smart, because you’re never that smart.
Bruce Halle

Everyone has an unbreakable contract to pay forward to the next customer, the next employee and the next generation.
Bruce Halle

Give everyone the benefit of the doubt.
Bruce Halle

Negative thoughts are a waste of time.
Bruce Halle

Be kind, and have a passion for humility.
Bruce Halle

[When he was in high school.] I remember thinking how lucky those guys were. Their parents had businesses.
Bruce Halle

[On his homeroom teacher] She had faith in me.
Bruce Halle

She just inspired me.
Bruce Halle

Keep your ideals close to your heart…
Bruce Halle

With your youth and energy go forth into this land…
Bruce Halle



This is America, the land of opportunity.
Bruce Halle

I’m a private in the Marine Corps and I’m making $18 a month…
Bruce Halle

Like a dutiful husband, I followed her into the store…
Bruce Halle

I think we were very blessed.
Bruce Halle

I think I had a normal childhood.
Bruce Halle

I have been blessed to have beautiful people around me.
Bruce Halle

Success comes from focusing on what each person owes to his family…
Bruce Halle

Work hard, be there on time…
Bruce Halle

[To his people at The Discount Tire Company] Do a good job and I’ll provide lifelong opportunity for you.
Bruce Halle

What can I do for you today?
Bruce Halle



The two worst people in the world to hire are friends and relatives.
Bruce Halle

We gave away geraniums, watermelons – anything for some goodwill.
Bruce Halle

When you get somebody’s else’s store, you are getting their reputation too.
Bruce Halle

I think I had a normal childhood. I can’t remember anything being hard about it, just a normal family growing up. What does it mean? Normal? There were no real big problems with my life as a child growing up. I just moved along and got older each year. No bad things happened.
Bruce Halle

We were little Catholic kids and we were growing up in a small town.
Bruce Halle

My dad was a fight kind of guy, so we always listened to fights.
Bruce Halle

[On his mother] She was a lady. She was a manager. She was the brains of the family.
Bruce Halle

Dad was a great guy, but Mom handled the finances and that kind of thing. Mom was charming, sweet and nice, where Dad was the hit man, the muscle.
Bruce Halle

Until he passed away, my dad always knelt at his bed at night and said prayers. He always did. Mom did too.
Bruce Halle

We had a beautiful childhood. There were no problems, no fights and no drunkenness, no swearing. It was a beautiful family. I think we were very blessed.
Bruce Halle
