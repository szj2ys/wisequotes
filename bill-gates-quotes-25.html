I don’t think people would object if someone pretended to be them to pay their traffic tickets.
Bill Gates

[In 1999] No consumer would stand in line for two hours to get service from private enterprise. Why should a plumber stand in line for two hours at a government office and lose two hours of pay, when by using the Internet he could get his license or pay his fees in a few minutes and be at work on time?
Bill Gates

Deregulation of telecommunications is probably the single greatest step that a country can take to create a digital economy.
Bill Gates

A fighter pilot can’t carry a laptop in flight. It might not stay in his lap while he’s pulling reverse Gs.
Bill Gates

Speed is of the essence in the chain of command. Disseminating orders fast can save a lot of lives.
Bill Gates

The rules of war haven’t changed. The victory goes to the side that can strike the quickest with the best intelligence.
Bill Gates

PCs can empower teachers and students more than any other group of knowledge workers.
Bill Gates

Most teachers have a great love of learning, and they’ll get excited about anything that will help kids learn. What teachers don’t want is to be thrown into something they have not had the opportunity to learn about and become comfortable with.
Bill Gates

[In 1999] The Internet solves the age-old problem of parents asking their children whether they have any homework and being unable to verify the answer then the children invariably say, ‘No.’
Bill Gates

A highly motivated student can learn from difficult reading materials, where a poorly motivated student needs accessible materials such as a video to learn.
Bill Gates



New software is helping students learn regardless of learning style or pace.
Bill Gates

Investments in technology should provide better information to every worker who might possibly use it.
Bill Gates

Every company can choose whether to lead or follow the emerging digital trends.
Bill Gates

Getting people motivated to take on responsibility is not a question of organizational structure so much as organizational attitude.
Bill Gates

It is impossible to manage a company totally from the center.
Bill Gates

It is impossible for a single person or single committee to stay on top of every issue in every business unit or subsidiary.
Bill Gates

Leaders need to provide strategy and direction and give employees tools that enable them to gather information and insight from around the world.
Bill Gates

Leaders shouldn’t try to make every decision.
Bill Gates

Empowerment of employees on the line requires smart machines at their fingertips.
Bill Gates

Economic eras were marked by long periods of stability followed by short periods of industry-wrenching change. Evolutionists would call this phenomenon punctuated equilibrium.
Bill Gates



Businesses have to react quickly to currency changes, new credit risks, and new valuations. Business decisions have to move at the pace of electronic markets.
Bill Gates

Information technology is the only way to have sufficiently quick reflexes connecting business strategy and organizational response.
Bill Gates

Human beings are not the biggest animals. We’re not the strongest or fastest. We’re not the sharpest in sight or smell. It’s amazing how we survived against the many fierce creatures of nature. We survived and prospered because of our brains.
Bill Gates

Our tools and technologies have helped us to shape the environment around us.
Bill Gates

[In 1999] I’d much rather be alive today than at any time in history – and not just because in an earlier age my skill set wouldn’t have been as valuable and I’d have been a prime candidate for some beast’s dinner.
Bill Gates

The tools of the Industrial Age extended the capabilities of our muscles. The tools of the digital age extend the capabilities of our minds.
Bill Gates

Citizens in every culture must engage on the social and political impact of digital technology to ensure that the new digital age reflects the society they want to create.
Bill Gates

Digital tools magnify the abilities that make us unique in the world: the ability to think, the ability to articulate our thoughts, the ability to work together to act on those thoughts.
Bill Gates

If companies empower their employees to solve problems and give them potent tools to do this with, they will always be amazed at how much creativity and initiative will blossom forth.
Bill Gates

A major shift in the computer industry has made end-to-end business solutions much more feasible.
Bill Gates



Horizontal integration makes for high volume and low price.
Bill Gates

The fundamental concept of components is that a programmer can use them without understanding their inner workings.
Bill Gates

Building a digital nervous system requires a well-defined framework for how to organize and roll out the computer hardware and network, how to make or buy applications, and how to operate the system on a daily basis.
Bill Gates

The system will one day be smart enough to arbitrate any conflicts between the new software and the software already on her machine.
Bill Gates

[In 1999] Moving away from the old applications is one of the toughest things for companies to do, but the transition is inevitable.
Bill Gates

The goal is to select projects that achieve the greatest reward in the shortest period of time.
Bill Gates

The successful companies select a few standards and enforce them strictly.
Bill Gates

The financial savings from standardizing on a single infrastructure can be very high.
Bill Gates

Your organization’s computing architecture should be a unifying design that maintains overall integration while enabling incremental change, especially at the departmental level.
Bill Gates

[On people thirty years ago in 1999.] They believed that the primary asset was the hardware and that the software was transitory. History has taught us the opposite. It’s the hardware that goes obsolete first, while software applications stay around seemingly forever…
Bill Gates
