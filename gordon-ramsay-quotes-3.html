Cooking is like having the most massive hard-on plus Viagra sprinkled on top of it and it’s still there 12 hour hours later.
Gordon Ramsay

I would run down along the Thames totally alone, watching the patterns on the water from the embankment and from the bridges. It’s so quiet when there’s not much traffic, a magical, beautiful experience.
Gordon Ramsay

Running gives me fresh air, freedom and quiet time to myself. It’s the best way of de-stressing.
Gordon Ramsay

Chef’s don’t sit and eat proper meals during the day – we graze – and that’s a bad way to live and eat. By running, I am able to enjoy eating without putting too much weight on.
Gordon Ramsay

You need to give it everything you have from the start…

Gordon Ramsay

What happened between me and my dad will never happen between me and my children.
Gordon Ramsay

Gordon Ramsay

Gordon Ramsay

Gordon Ramsay

I don’t think customers are tough enough.
Gordon Ramsay

Gordon Ramsay

I will only start worrying if my customers start complaining.
Gordon Ramsay

Don’t judge me, judge my food.
Gordon Ramsay

This month I have served Kylie Minogue, George Clooney and my little sister. They all got the same treatment as every other customer.
Gordon Ramsay

Any time a good new restaurant opens up, anywhere in the world, we check it out. Five years ago, we could afford to do this just in Britain. But now, to stay in front, we have to respond to the amazing changes happening in say, Asia and Australia… Then we start incorporating the best ideas into what we do.
Gordon Ramsay

[In response to whether he does all the cooking in all of his restaurants.] Is that an Armani suit you are wearing? [‘Yes it is.’] Cost about five hundred quid? [No, $2,500.] That’ a lot of money for a suit. When you bought it, did you ask if it was f*cking Giorgio who did all the stitching. Next question.
Gordon Ramsay

It’s always been my ambition to come to America and open a restaurant. But you’ve only got one shot at it, so you have to get it right.
Gordon Ramsay



Eating out can be a pain in the arse.
Gordon Ramsay

I could never take my children and spend 22 pounds a head just to sit and have afternoon tea, it’s not apt, it’s wrong and I can’t think any differently. My mum would be mortified.
Gordon Ramsay

I haven’t lost the plot and I don’t want anyone else to either.
Gordon Ramsay

We have a vegetarian menu in all my restaurants now…
Gordon Ramsay

I am still learning – that’s the beauty of my food, I’m still getting better. I still consider myself a cook but it takes years to become a great chef.
Gordon Ramsay

[On what he would like to come back as in his next life.] Prime Minister.
Gordon Ramsay

I’ve just kept my head down, my mouth shut.
Gordon Ramsay

I’ve been at the coalface for 20 years, which you can probably tell by the depth of the lines on my face.
Gordon Ramsay



I cook, I create, I’m incredibly excited by what I do.
Gordon Ramsay

[In November 2009] I’ve still got a lot to achieve.
Gordon Ramsay

Gordon Ramsay

Gordon Ramsay

Gordon Ramsay

I’m a fighter. I will fight to the last minute.

Work is who I am, who I want to be. I sometimes think that if I were to stop working, I’d stop existing.
