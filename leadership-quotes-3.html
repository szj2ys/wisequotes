Harold Geneen

He is a catalyst, and though things would not get done as well if he weren't there, when they succeed he takes no credit. And because he takes no credit, credit never leaves him.
Lao-Tzu

Managers prefer to work with people, while leaders arouse emotion in people.
Abraham Zalisnick

I start with premise that the function of leadership is to produce more leaders, not more followers.
Ralph Nader

Lao-Tzu

I never associate passion with the word manager, and I’ve never seen a leader without it.
Jack Welch

Good business leaders create a vision, articulate the vision, passionately own the vision, and relentlessly drive it to completion.
Jack Welch

I firmly believe my job is to walk around with a can of water in one hand and a can of fertilizer in the other and to make things flourish.
Jack Welch

George S Patton

A leader has to be ahead of his men. You’ve got to know what is going on all the time.
George S Patton

Donald Trump

Graham Turner

To muster his host and bring it into danger- this may be termed the business of the general.
