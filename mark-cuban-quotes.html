Always run your business like you are going to be competing with biggest technology companies in your industry – Google, Facebook, Oracle, Microsoft, whomever. They may not be your direct competitors. They may be a vendor. They may be a direct competitor and a vendor. Whatever they may be to your business, if you are in the technology business in any shape or form, you have to anticipate that you will have to compete with them at some point. I ask myself every week what I would do if they entered any of my businesses. If you are ready to compete with the big guys, you are ready to compete with anyone else.
Mark Cuban

Watching the best taught me how to run my businesses.
Mark Cuban



I love to compete. I always have.
Mark Cuban

I had so many jobs my parents wondered if I would ever be stable.
Mark Cuban

In every job, I would justify in my mind, whether I loved it or hated it, that I was getting paid to learn and every experience would be of value when I figured out what I wanted to do when I grew up.
Mark Cuban

It is time to get paid to learn.
Mark Cuban

[In May 2012] I always laugh at all the pundits /analysts who try to tell you what any non dividend paying stock is worth.
Mark Cuban

Mark Cuban
