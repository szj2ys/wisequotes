Kim Kiyosaki

Kim Kiyosaki

Kim Kiyosaki

Kim Kiyosaki

Kim Kiyosaki

We just kept going and didn’t quit!
Kim Kiyosaki

Kim Kiyosaki

We struggled for a good year and a half.
Kim Kiyosaki

That time of being homeless and broke, was a very difficult time. In retrospect, it was probably one of the best things that could have happened to us.
