Carroll Shelby

Carroll Shelby

You better learn to work with people because they’re the only thing you have to make anything work.

That’s all business is, it’s just solving problems everyday.
Carroll Shelby

Carroll Shelby

[At age 85] Now that I have macular degeneration I don’t drive quite as fast…

[On having been through 40 heart attacks] I thought it was just angina…
Carroll Shelby

I never looked back…

The guys in the shop want to go racing.
Carroll Shelby

Break the sh*t out of it. This is the first car. You’re going to break every damned thing in the car in testing. You have to in order to know if it’s any damned good.
Carroll Shelby

It is never easy to build a sports car with a major manufacturer.
Carroll Shelby
