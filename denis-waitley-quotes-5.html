If I had two wishes, I know what they would be I’d wish for Roots to cling to, and Wings to set me free;
Denis Waitley

Roots of inner values, like rings within a tree, and Wings of independence to seek my destiny.
Denis Waitley

Roots to hold forever to keep me safe and strong, To let me know you love me, when I’ve done something wrong;
Denis Waitley

To show me by example, and help me learn to choose, To take those actions every day to win instead of lose.
Denis Waitley

Just be there when I need you, to tell me it’s all right, To face my fear of falling when I test my wings in flight;
Denis Waitley

Don’t make my life too easy, it’s better if I try, And fail and get back up myself, so I can learn to fly.
Denis Waitley

If I had two wishes, and two were all I had, And they could just be granted, by my Mom and Dad; I wouldn’t ask for money or any store-bought things,
Denis Waitley

The greatest gifts I’d ask for are simply Roots and Wings.
Denis Waitley

A good life is a collection of happy memories.
Denis Waitley

Each human being on earth has equal rights to fulfill his or her own potential.
Denis Waitley



You are an uncut gemstone of priceless value. Cut and polish your potential with knowledge, skills and service and you will be in great demand throughout your life.
Denis Waitley

View stumbling blocks as stepping stones to the stars.
Denis Waitley

Optimism creates energy and is contagious.
Denis Waitley

Put up the dream. Put in the knowledge. Put out the effort.
Denis Waitley

The two greatest fear busters are knowledge and action.
Denis Waitley

You can’t concentrate on the reverse of an idea. A fear is a goal moving in the opposite direction from your desire.
Denis Waitley

We can change if we want to.
Denis Waitley

Think of your imagination as a skill rather than a talent and learn to use it.
Denis Waitley

Motivation is an inner force that compels behavior. Your inner drives will propel you further and faster than external perks.
Denis Waitley

Marriage is not total agreement. It is looking in the same direction together.
Denis Waitley



Listening without bias or distraction is the greatest value you can pay another person.
Denis Waitley

Denis Waitley
