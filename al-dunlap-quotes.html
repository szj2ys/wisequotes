(Albert J Dunlap Quotes, Al Chainsaw Dunlap Quotes)

The success of a corporation is inversely proportional to the size and opulence of its headquarters.
Al Dunlap

You’re not in business to be liked.
Al Dunlap

Dunlap = 1. To turn a company around at lightning speed. 2. To focus on the best; to eliminate what is not the best 3. To protect and enhance shareholder value.
Al Dunlap

Business is dynamic. If it doesn’t change, it becomes obsolete.
Al Dunlap

Run a tight, money-making ship today, and you can sail the world tomorrow.
Al Dunlap

If you go with the flow, you’ll ultimately drown in the undertow.
Al Dunlap

A business plan is like a laser, not a shotgun.
Al Dunlap

If you set too many goals, you will fail.
Al Dunlap

An effective business management strategy is like a workout. If I work out regularly, I get fit. If I stay with the workouts, I’ll get fitter every year. But if I just work on my arms this year, my legs next year, I might not live long enough to get fit.
Al Dunlap

If you operate by consensus, you’re not making hard decisions.
Al Dunlap



When there are problems, I don’t blame employees. I pick the right targets: management and the board of directors.
Al Dunlap

Today, in good times and bad, everyone must be a turnaround manager.
Al Dunlap

Cost is always your enemy.
Al Dunlap

If you’re in business, you’re in business for one thing – to make money.
Al Dunlap

I’m probably a much nicer guy than most people think, but who’s going to hire a nice guy to turn around a failing conglomerate?
Al Dunlap

There’s nothing better than being handed a ton of clay and assigned to mold and shape it into a beautiful piece of art.
Al Dunlap

Management is the spine around which all the rest takes shape.
Al Dunlap

I cannot keep the people who created the debacle I’m expected to fix.
Al Dunlap

Never cut an hourly worker before you deal with the headquarters staff.
Al Dunlap

Don’t let the people who buy raw materials and supplies for you constantly lunch and fraternize with suppliers. They may feel like they work for the suppliers.
Al Dunlap



Employees should never become too comfortable with suppliers.
Al Dunlap

You can make more money buying right than you can possibly make selling right.
Al Dunlap

To create shareholder value, period.
Al Dunlap

Boards should be absolutely certain that the company is run properly from a fiduciary standpoint in every degree. I am a great believer in the audit committee having full access to the auditors in every way, shape and form.
Al Dunlap

Nicknames are part of image, not strategy.
Al Dunlap

What I do: Make a company and the people who work there the best!
Al Dunlap

I’m not an academic or consultant making a living just by studying problems.
Al Dunlap

It’s no longer enough to be competent or pretty good. You must be great.
Al Dunlap

The most important person in any company is the shareholder.
Al Dunlap

Everyone in a company needs incentives, but they should be tied directly to the performance of the company.
Al Dunlap



Don’t turn decision making over to an army of consultants.
Al Dunlap

Put your money where your reputation is, and have the utmost faith in your own talent.
Al Dunlap

Be outrageous – but pick your spots carefully.
Al Dunlap

You’re not in business to be liked… We’re here to succeed.
Al Dunlap

If you want a friend, get a dog. I’m not taking any chances; I’ve got two dogs.
Al Dunlap

Create a strategy, follow through, take risks, sacrifice, recognize opportunity, and pick up the winner’s spoils at the end.
Al Dunlap

The price of leadership is criticism.
Al Dunlap

Magnify your own abilities by surrounding yourself with great people.
Al Dunlap

Focus like a laser.
Al Dunlap

Envision the future and plan a route to it.
Al Dunlap
