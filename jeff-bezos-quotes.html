Jeff Bezos

Jeff Bezos

Jeff Bezos

Jeff Bezos

Jeff Bezos

Trust is hard-earned, easily lost.

Jeff Bezos

Even the most durable competitive advantages fade with time if you don't replace them with new advantages.
Jeff Bezos

One of the best durable advantages is a good consumer brand. History has shown this to be true.
Jeff Bezos

[In January 2013] One of the things that’s very hard to believe when you’re 22 or 23 years old is that you don’t already know everything.
Jeff Bezos

You can invent your way out of any box if you believe that you can.
Jeff Bezos

You can’t ask for trust, you just have to do it the hard way, one step at a time.
Jeff Bezos

Do something you’re very passionate about, and don’t try to chase what is kind of the ‘hot passion’ of the day.
Jeff Bezos

People don’t want gadgets… they want services.
Jeff Bezos

[In July 2010] Our mission is earth’s most customer-centric company.
Jeff Bezos

If you’re going to invent new things you’ve also got to be able - this goes along with long-term orientation, you’ve got to be able to endure a lot of criticism.
Jeff Bezos



Nobody else has to fail for us to do well.
Jeff Bezos

[In July 2010] Sometimes I think we try to solve problems before we understand the problem.
Jeff Bezos

I didn't think I'd regret trying and failing… I suspected I would always be haunted by a decision to not try at all.
Jeff Bezos

I took the less safe path to follow my passion, and I'm proud of that choice.
Jeff Bezos

Jeff Bezos

Being customer-focused allows you to be more pioneering.
Jeff Bezos

We are swinging for the fences at Amazon. You should expect mistakes.
Jeff Bezos

You don't want to have to reinvent the wheel every time.
Jeff Bezos

Jeff Bezos

The rate of change is getting faster and faster.
Jeff Bezos

Jeff Bezos

Jeff Bezos

Jeff Bezos
