The other fellow's mistakes are a weak alibi for your own.
Napoleon Hill

Failure is not a disgrace if you have sincerely done your best
Napoleon Hill

If you have no major purpose, you are drifting toward certain failure.
Napoleon Hill

Don't blame children who are bad. Blame those who failed to discipline them.
Napoleon Hill

Human faults are like garden weeds. They grow without cultivation and soon take over the place if they aren't thinned out.
Napoleon Hill

Remember that the faults of man are pretty evenly distributed among all of us.
Napoleon Hill

Self-pity is an opiate.
Napoleon Hill

A wise man watches his faults more closely than his virtues; others reverse the order.
Napoleon Hill

Failure is a blessing when it pushes one out of a cushioned seat of self-satisfaction and forces him to do something useful.
Napoleon Hill

Failure seems to be nature's plan for preparing men for great responsibilities.
Napoleon Hill



Your failure may prove to be an asset, provided you know why you failed.
Napoleon Hill

If you don't know why you failed, you are no wiser than when you began.
Napoleon Hill

Napoleon Hill

The best way to get favors is to start handing out favors.
Napoleon Hill

Faith cannot be created, but it can be appropriated by all who have prepared their minds to receive it
Napoleon Hill

Napoleon Hill

No man can destroy your faith in anything unless you consent.
Napoleon Hill

The greatest of all miracles is the power of simple faith.
Napoleon Hill

Misfortune seldom tangles with the man whose constant bodyguards are hope and faith.
Napoleon Hill

Faith needs a foundation on which to stand. Fear exists without a base.
Napoleon Hill



Faith is born of definiteness of purpose operating in a positive mental attitude.
Napoleon Hill

Faith is a state of mind that often makes the word "impossible" obsolete.
Napoleon Hill

Faith will not bring you what you desire, but it will show you the way to go after it for yourself.
Napoleon Hill

Loud threats often indicate deep fears.
Napoleon Hill

Defeat will respect you more if you learn to accept it without fear!
Napoleon Hill

Don't temporize with fear, just go ahead and kill it.
Napoleon Hill

Napoleon Hill

Fear is the devil's greatest weapon and man's greatest enemy.
Napoleon Hill

Fear is the most costly of all the human emotions, although most fears have no foundation in facts.
Napoleon Hill

Men with a clear conscience seldom fear anything.
Napoleon Hill

Napoleon Hill

Napoleon Hill
