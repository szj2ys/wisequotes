Ralph Lauren Quotes

100 Ralph Lauren Quotes (Ralph Lifshitz Quotes)

1 2 3



I had talent. I wanted to be my own boss. I just had to.
Ralph Lauren

I don’t design to design. I design into living.
Ralph Lauren

My goal was always to be myself.
Ralph Lauren

People want what I want.
Ralph Lauren

People are realizing what makes them feel good.
Ralph Lauren

People who come to shop want to be entertained.
Ralph Lauren

When I think something is out there too long, I get off it, even if it’s hot, even if it’s very, very popular.
Ralph Lauren

You can’t just sit still.
Ralph Lauren

I didn’t want to be one of many but the only one.
Ralph Lauren

There was no sense I would ever be a fashion designer.
Ralph Lauren



I always wanted to be the star of the movie, but I never thought I’d be a movie star.
Ralph Lauren

Clothes were not my whole life. I think I just, at a very early age, looked cool.
Ralph Lauren

I’ve never had a sense of flying high…
Ralph Lauren

We didn’t want an English house or an American house. We wanted something timeless…
Ralph Lauren

You have to look for things you really love, for what your dream is.
Ralph Lauren

I have the most beautiful store in the world. To open a store in an ugly building across the street – I have to be nuts.
Ralph Lauren

I am copied by everyone in the world.
Ralph Lauren

Cars today all look the same.
Ralph Lauren

I have a lot of clothes, but they’re all sort of [laughs] the same.
Ralph Lauren

When I came into the business, there were manufacturers but no designers…
Ralph Lauren



[In 1973] Katharine Hepburn in boots, jodhpurs and a sweater, with her hair flowing – that’s the look.
Ralph Lauren

I have tried to be authentic in everything I do.
Ralph Lauren

If you are out there, you must want to be.
Ralph Lauren

I did my own thing.
Ralph Lauren

I felt like I was a leader, not a follower.
Ralph Lauren

I had a lot of friends, but I was always individual…
Ralph Lauren

The real excitement of what you’re doing is in the doing of it.
Ralph Lauren

I’ve been weathered, but I don’t feel like I’m over.
Ralph Lauren

The key to longevity is to keep doing what you do better than anyone else. We work real hard at that. It’s about getting your message out to the consumer. It’s about getting their trust, but also getting them excited, again and again.
Ralph Lauren

I don’t design clothes, I design dreams.
Ralph Lauren



A leader has the vision and conviction that a dream can be achieved. He inspires the power and energy to get it done.
Ralph Lauren

I always wanted to make movies, but I’m just doing them in a different way, creating the dreams, the dream lifestyle. My life helps me with my job, and my job helps me with my life.
Ralph Lauren

[On a model of the 10,000 square foot Polo Sport Emporium at 888 Madison opening in 1993] I hope the store doesn’t look like this.
Ralph Lauren

[In 1993] To be a classic in this day and age is a real challenge.
Ralph Lauren

It’s the model of the store. It’s not what the store’s gonna look like.
Ralph Lauren

Every time I look at the store, I say, ‘Did I made a mistake?’. I took this store two years ago, when retailing was a disaster. Everything was going down. Everyone was worried about businesses closing. My business wasn’t flying through the roof, either. I have the most beautiful store in the world. To open a store in an ugly building across the street – I have to be nuts.
Ralph Lauren

Yes, I own things, but that’s not what I’m about. It’s not meant for everybody to see. It’s not laid out for parties.
Ralph Lauren

It’s right there in front of you… I was the guy walking into the store. My wife was the girl. My kids were the kids. It was always very personal. It wasn’t industry. The fashion industry never cared.
Ralph Lauren

Americans have an insecurity complex about themselves. I spawned an industry. America didn’t look like this. There was no Banana Republic, no Gap, no J. Crew. Go back and see where fashion was when I started. I am copied by everyone in the world.
Ralph Lauren

There’s a heritage behind my designs, but there’s also a newness and excitement that I inject.
Ralph Lauren
