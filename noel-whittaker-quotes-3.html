Any fool with money can write a cheque, pick up bills for smashed cars or pay for expensive toys. If life was as simple as that, all the wealthy people in the world would have perfect children.
Noel Whittaker

Children seldom listen to what their parents say, but they usually will imitate what they do.
Noel Whittaker

Children are one of the greatest joys in life. Should you help them? Of course you must. Just do it in a way that will help them to turn their market cart into a chariot of the sun.
Noel Whittaker

Everything has its price.
Noel Whittaker

One of the main differences between the financial winners and the financial losers is that the former borrow at fairly low rates of interest to buy assets such as houses that rise in value. The latter pay horrendous rates of interest to buy consumer items such as furniture, or worse still holidays, that have no permanent value.
Noel Whittaker

One of the secrets of success in life is to ask yourself the right questions.
Noel Whittaker

Decide to be a ‘have’ – not a ‘have-not.’
Noel Whittaker

One of my favourite stories is about the passenger on the Titanic whose last words were, ‘If I’d known the ship was going to sink I would have had a second helping of dessert.’
Noel Whittaker

Having a high income is not the same as building up assets.
Noel Whittaker

Everything worthwhile takes time.



Life is like planting seeds. If you keep planting ‘seeds’ all the time some never come up, some flourish, some go well for a while then die off, and others lie dormant for years and then suddenly take off.
Noel Whittaker

Make it a habit to help anybody who crosses our path…
Noel Whittaker

A happy life is a collection of happy days…
Noel Whittaker

A fortune is simply a collection of a lot of little sums of money.
Noel Whittaker

One of the greatest fallacies of all time is that success suddenly arrives at your door.
Noel Whittaker

There’s an old saying, ‘It’s easier to keep a car out of a bog than to dig it out once it’s in there’…
Noel Whittaker

Once you become committed to your goal, you will find that the right people and situations will start coming into your life.
Noel Whittaker

Retirement should mean the freedom to be yourself, not a time to do nothing.
Noel Whittaker

Why waste time and money on chasing illusions when you can become wealthy using the tried and true?
Noel Whittaker

92% of people will NEVER make the sacrifices needed to be successful, so there is very little competition for anybody who is prepared to go the extra mile.
Noel Whittaker



You have but one life – make sure you do something with it!
Noel Whittaker
