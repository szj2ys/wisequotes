[In 2011] We are assiduous in ensuring that the property and assets of the Museum are being properly and effectively administered.
John Gandel

[In 2011 on the Jewish Museum of Australia] The property and assets of the Museum are fully safeguarded…
John Gandel

[On the Jewish Museum of Australia in 2011] The variety and sheer number of people who work tirelessly for the organisation make it impossible for me to thank them all.
John Gandel

[On the Jewish Museum of Australia in 2011] We urge the community to support the Museum in order to sustain and enhance such a vital organisation.
John Gandel

[On the Computer for Every Child project in 2002] I haven’t pushed it in Australia yet.
John Gandel

[In September 2010] Considering we already have $800m invested in CFS Retail, I felt that was enough to put into the company.
John Gandel

[On a strong takeup of an institutional placement of CFS Retail with demand being nearly double the units issued] It’s a good result for the shares.
John Gandel

[On the fragile state of the US economy in September 2010 with its heavy deficit] If you were a private individual your bank would say ‘you are on the way to bankruptcy’.
John Gandel

[On Australian retail in September 2010] Retail sales are not terrible, but they are inconsistent.
John Gandel

[On the $280m expansion of Chadstone Shopping Centre in 2009 bringing in key global brands] That doesn’t exist in another shopping centre in Australia, only in downtown areas… and perhaps Bondi Junction.
John Gandel



[On shopping centres] Centres have to be seamless. You can’t have better and lesser areas.
John Gandel

CFS are creating a new direction, where there are not many people doing it well.
John Gandel

[On the Simon Property Group] Simon are pretty smart.
John Gandel

[On his 15% investment in Charter Hall in September 2010] We see ourselves doing more (with Charter Hall) when it suits both parties, and having in mind shareholders are the primary focus of all directors of Charter Hall.
John Gandel

[On the overall Centro management business in September 2010] It’s a hard one, it’s a really hard one. I spoke to some of the banks three years ago and said you should have sold.
John Gandel

[On the Gandel Holocaust Studies Program for Australian Educators in December 2012] There is an extremely important initiative and we are delighted to be involved. The participants will come home to actively involve Holocaust studies in their work and raise awareness of the Holocaust within their communities. We believe their visit to Israel and attendance at the Yad Vashem course will be a highlight of their professional careers and their life in general.
John Gandel

Seize the moment.
John Gandel

The recognition of an unusual opportunity…
John Gandel

Action a carefully thought out plan.
John Gandel

For me, giving is an obligation.
John Gandel
