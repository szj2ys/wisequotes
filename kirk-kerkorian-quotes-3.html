[On someone saying it would be very hard for me to work at a place in Las Vegas without the union] I don’t get involved in any of those things. I leave it all up to the management.
Kirk Kerkorian

[On asking a security guard who didn’t recognize him on looking at the MGM Grand Garden during construction] Couldn’t we just take a peek?
Kirk Kerkorian

[On the MGM Grand Hotel and Theme Park opening on December 18, 1993] I think it’s a completely new convention across the board for families.
Kirk Kerkorian

Steve isn’t interested. I’m just going to sell my stock.
Kirk Kerkorian

[On the MGM lion logo] Are you familiar with this lion’s roar?
Kirk Kerkorian

It would be a fantastic move for your career. We’re all excited about Rain Man, but you could take the history of MGM and grow it into something even bigger than anything you’ve ever done.
Kirk Kerkorian

[On his father – he was] A big, rough man who didn’t take anything from anybody.
Kirk Kerkorian
