Most of our best ideas for improving the business start with people ringing me up, especially franchisees.
Jim Penman

It’s weird being a kind of mythical figure.
Jim Penman

I have to beat down people’s expectations. I’m just a normal guy, probably far less capable than they are in many ways.
Jim Penman

Having a reasonable sense of proportion about yourself is vitally important and it’s good for business.
Jim Penman

You can’t afford to be an egotist. It can damage you and your business.
Jim Penman

No one is irreplaceable. If they are, maybe things should be done differently.
Jim Penman

I warm to people who want to achieve something meaningful in life, people with drive, ambition and a passion for success.
Jim Penman

One of the great things about franchising is that you tend to mix with great people.
Jim Penman

I get nostalgic when I smell cut grass.
Jim Penman

Jim Penman



Investing money creates a commitment that makes someone far more likely to succeed.
Jim Penman

The challenge of prosperity is not to become soft or complacent…
Jim Penman

Jim Penman
