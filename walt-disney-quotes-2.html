Walt Disney

Walt Disney

Walt Disney

Walt Disney

Walt Disney

Walt Disney

Walt Disney

Walt Disney

Walt Disney

Walt Disney

Walt Disney

He had himself a trailer, and he used to go out and go fishing, and he didn’t worry about payrolls and stories and picture grosses or anything. And he was the happy one. I always said, ‘He’s the smart Disney.’
Walt Disney

[On Mickey Mouse] Born of necessity, the little fellow literally freed us of immediate worry.
Walt Disney

All we ever intended for him or expected of him was that he should continued to make people everywhere chuckle with him and at him.
Walt Disney

I can’t believe that there are any heights that can’t be scaled by a man who knows the secret of making dreams come true.
Walt Disney

When you believe in a thing, believe in it all the way, implicitly and unquestionably.
Walt Disney

Fantasy, if it’s really convincing, can’t become dated…
Walt Disney



Deeds rather than words express my concept of the part religion should play in everyday life.
Walt Disney

I didn’t treat my own youngsters like fragile flowers, and I think no parent should.
Walt Disney

Life is composed of lights and shadow, and we would be untruthful, insincere, and saccharine if we tried to pretend there were no shadows.
Walt Disney

The important thing is to teach a child that good can always triumph over evil…
Walt Disney

Lecturing to children is no answer to delinquency.
Walt Disney

Preaching won’t keep youngsters out of trouble, but keeping their minds occupied will.
Walt Disney

Wisdom and courage are enough to defeat big, bad wolves of every description, and send them slinking away all huffed and puffed out.
Walt Disney

Walt Disney

Walt Disney

Walt Disney

Walt Disney

Walt Disney

Walt Disney
