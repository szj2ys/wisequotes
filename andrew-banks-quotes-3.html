Andrew Banks

[In June 2011.] Organisations have to be very flexible and adapt.

[In August 2011 on Australia’s domestic conditions.] To the pessimist’s who say that unemployment will rise here sharply, I don’t agree. You’ve got to look at the demographics and in Australia we’ve got a record number of people now moving towards retirement so the participation rates are pretty low. And unless someone dramatically increases immigration, I think there’ll still be dramatic skill shortages in this country.
Andrew Banks
