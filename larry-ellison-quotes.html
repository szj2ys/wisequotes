Larry Ellison Quotes

302 Larry Ellison Quotes (Oracle, Lawrence Joseph Ellison)

1 2



In marketing simple messages always win.
Larry Ellison

It never ceases to amaze me how the product name can be the difference between success and failure in the technology industry.
Larry Ellison

In a great company everybody sells – not just the salespeople.
Larry Ellison

When you innovate, you’ve got to be prepared for everyone telling you you’re nuts.
Larry Ellison

Life is the enlightened pursuit of happiness, not the unenlightened pursuit of as much money as you can accumulate.
Larry Ellison

We work because work is an act of creation. We identify with it… I look at the company and I think, ‘This is me.’
Larry Ellison

What motivated me was the desire to control my environment…
Larry Ellison

Built it once and sell it over and over again.
Larry Ellison

The bigger the apparent risk, the fewer people will try to go there.
Larry Ellison

Being first to finish is what it’s all about.
Larry Ellison



When you find errors in conventional wisdom – when everyone says A and A is not true – that is when you gain competitive advantage. Only a few times do you have to find errors in conventional wisdom to make a living.
Larry Ellison

I'm addicted to winning. The more you win, the more you want to win
Larry Ellison

A lot of what keeps me going and keeps my drive is I'm curious as to how far I can go...
Larry Ellison

Everyone who works hard, maybe a little cleverly, has the opportunity to make almost anything possible.
Larry Ellison

You have to find places to take advantage, and the way to have the advantage is to find flaws in the conventional strategy.
Larry Ellison

My original goal was to build a company doing about ten million dollars of revenue and employing about fifty people. What motivated me was the desire to control my environment so I wouldn’t have to do things I didn’t want to do or spend time with people I didn’t enjoy working with.
Larry Ellison

[In September 1998] Oracle is a $4.8 billion market cap company today because we've always gone against convention.
Larry Ellison

When we hit the wall, we hit it very, very hard.
Larry Ellison

[To Adda Quinn his first wife.] If you stay with me, I will become a millionaire.
Larry Ellison

Steve [Jobs] created the only lifestyle brand in the tech industry. There are cars people are proud to have – Porsche, Ferrari, Prius – because what I drive says something about me. People feel the same way about an Apple product. [On someone suggesting that Gates was brilliant.] Bill Gates, brilliant? Really?… I think Bill is a very bright guy, but I don’t think his strength is in his intellect at all. It’s in his relentlessness.
Larry Ellison



We’d sell anything anyone wanted to buy.
Larry Ellison

[On the importance of getting marketshare early.] How much does it cost Pepsi to get one half of one percent of the market from Coke once the market has been established? It’s very expensive.
Larry Ellison

[On his company’s early approach method to customers.] Here’s our software. Use it. I dare you.
Larry Ellison

In our business, with the development of any new technology, there’s tremendous uncertainty.
Larry Ellison

I pissed a lot of people off… Still do.
Larry Ellison

The only things that are important in our lives are love and work – not necessarily in that order, but they’re both important.
Larry Ellison

People I care about, people I love, are essential for my sanity to make it through every day.
Larry Ellison

Work and love, conspire to deliver some kind of happiness.
Larry Ellison

[In July 1999] If you look at Apple how much difference can one person make?… I think one person can make a tremendous difference.
Larry Ellison

[In July 1999] Apple with Steve Jobs [Finger going up a graph.] Apple without Steve Jobs [Finger going down a graph.]
Larry Ellison



Even if you’re ready to sell your soul, you usually find that nobody’s buying.
Larry Ellison

When we say there’s a training problem, it’s usually because a product is too difficult to use. If you can use Yahoo! Or Amazon without any training, you should be able to use most business software with little or no training. Unfortunately, our engineers usually stop short of making our products as easy to use as possible.
Larry Ellison

Don’t tell us how you’ve been running your business for the last twenty years. Instead, let’s try to figure out how you want to run your business for the next twenty years.
Larry Ellison

I never had the discipline to do things I didn’t like.
Larry Ellison

I was good at logic.
Larry Ellison

I only ever picked the high-risk approach when I thought that it would increase our chance of winning and our share of reward.
Larry Ellison

Whenever you travel an untraveled path there’s risk.
Larry Ellison

Oracle rode the Internet wave because our database powered virtually every major Web site on the Net.
Larry Ellison

[On doing the Sydney to Hobart yacht race in 1998.] I wasn’t ‘traumatized’ by the race, but I sure didn’t enjoy it. I’d have to be an idiot to spend any more of my Christmas holidays throwing up on a sailboat.
Larry Ellison

[On selling one of his first sailing boats.] I finally had to accept that eating came before sailing in Maslow’s hierarchy of needs.
Larry Ellison
