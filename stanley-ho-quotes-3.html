[At the age of 83] I must believe that sports has brought me this good health, only sports. I swim everyday and I did a lot of walking, I do a lot of dancing…
Stanley Ho

I have really many many good friends, real sincere friends
Stanley Ho

[On friends in 2004] You have to weigh it very carefully whether they are my real friends or whether they are after my money.
Stanley Ho

[On whether he seeks advice from his friends] I think it's the other way around, they normally ask me for advice.
Stanley Ho

[On his life in 2004] I have never been happier in my life during these few years. I think China has been most liberal to both Hong Kong and Macau. We should enjoy this freedom, this so called one country, two systems, it is working very well in Macau and I hope in due time Hong Kong will do the same.
Stanley Ho

[In January 2011] For the past 10 years I have very much loved my family and I have never had to take any action of you sueing me and me sueing you.
Stanley Ho

[On Sheldon Adelson and Steve Wynn coming into the Macau marketplace] The success of one market model cannot be migrated to another. Ignoring Macau’s special characteristics and duplicating a Las Vegas or an Atlantic City would not be a successful strategy.
Stanley Ho

[In August 2009] Fit and healthy as I am, I have no plans to retire.
Stanley Ho

Macau is limited in size and land is expensive, so gaming and leisure development must be more efficient. We follow a very conservative financial strategy. No matter how thorny the problem is, I will find a way through.
Stanley Ho

[On children Lawrence and Pansy not teaming up to quash the opposition] They are my children but also my competitors.
Stanley Ho



I want to make it very big.
Stanley Ho

[On family share transfers in January 2011] They forced me to sign…
Stanley Ho

[On family disputes on share transfers] The whole thing is in a mess.
Stanley Ho

[At age 89 on a tv statement in which he said he was changing his lawyer but not really] I was under pressure.
Stanley Ho

[To his lawyer on legal action with family] What you have done mainly to scare them, to change their mind… and to surrender… all the shares back to me.
Stanley Ho

It is especially meaningful to receive this award from the industry from which I have enjoyed this long association.
Stanley Ho

[The view] that our enterprise is merely one of a gambling character [was] really a misunderstanding and even a misconception.
Stanley Ho

Our purpose everywhere is to bring a new prosperity to Macau and improve the welfare and living standards of it’s citizens.
Stanley Ho

In 1993 the number of visitors exceeded 2 million for the first time. Gaming since then has become the major contributor of Macau’s tax revenue and also one of the largest employers.
Stanley Ho

How many decades can a man live?
Stanley Ho



No matter how thorny the problem is, I will find a way through.
Stanley Ho
