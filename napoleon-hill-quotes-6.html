The man who fears poverty will never be rich.
Napoleon Hill

Fear kills more people than do most things which men fear.
Napoleon Hill

You needn't fear the man who fears himself, unless you are that man.
Napoleon Hill

Faith needs a foundation on which to stand. Fear exists without a base.
Napoleon Hill

The man who calls on his friends only when he goes after something soon finds himself without friends.
Napoleon Hill

Friends must be grown to order—not taken for granted.
Napoleon Hill

If you wish "acquaintanceship," be rich. If you wish friends, be a friend.
Napoleon Hill

If you must let someone down, be sure it isn't the friend who helped you up when you were down.
Napoleon Hill

A friend is one who knows all about you and still respects you.
Napoleon Hill

Friendship needs frequent expression to remain alive.
Napoleon Hill



Friendship recognizes faults in friends but does not speak of them.
Napoleon Hill

Some men "gripe" when they have a just cause and others just gripe.
Napoleon Hill

It is better to earn a promotion than to gripe for it.
Napoleon Hill

If you must gripe in order to be happy, in heaven's name do it in a whisper so you'll not disturb others.
Napoleon Hill

Don't be too hard on the fellow who is always griping, for he is making life pretty tough for himself as it is.
Napoleon Hill

If you must gripe, why not gripe for a bigger opportunity to be useful to others?
Napoleon Hill

Whine about your misfortunes and thereby multiply them, but keep still and starve them out.
Napoleon Hill

Give a griper plenty of rope and he will hang himself without your help.
Napoleon Hill

When you feel sluggish, try nature's doctor. Just quit eating until you are hungry again.
Napoleon Hill

The best time to "doctor" is before you become sick.
Napoleon Hill



If you would have good health, learn to quit eating before you are entirely satisfied.
Napoleon Hill

Keep your mind on your physical ills and you will always be sick. Ditto for health.
Napoleon Hill

Some athletes endorse cigarettes, for a price, but they don't smoke them at any price.
Napoleon Hill

Ripe fruit and raw vegetables constitute a healthful diet of which one can never overeat.
Napoleon Hill

You know what to feed your automobile for good service; now learn what to feed yourself for good health.
Napoleon Hill

Don't try to cure a headache. It's better to cure the thing that caused it.
Napoleon Hill

A big appetite doesn't always lead to sound health.
Napoleon Hill

Do you have the cigarette habit, or does it have you?
Napoleon Hill

Pills will not cure toxic poisoning, but plenty of water will.
Napoleon Hill

Proper diet and elimination will serve better than an apple a day to keep the "medico" away.
Napoleon Hill



Watch your eating habits and save a doctor's bill.
Napoleon Hill

A blind boy paid his way to a master's degree at Northwestern University by taking notes on class lectures in the Braille system of shorthand, writing them out on a typewriter, and selling copies to his classmates who had stronger eyes but weak ambition.
Napoleon Hill

If you become discouraged, think of Helen Keller who, although deaf, dumb, and blind, made a good living by writing books to inspire her more fortunate fellowmen.
Napoleon Hill

The man who starts at the top is greatly handicapped because then he can move in but one direction - downward!
Napoleon Hill

Napoleon Hill

Happiness can be multiplied by sharing it with others without diminishing the original source. It is the one asset which increases when it is given away.
Napoleon Hill

A smile is a little thing that may produce big results.
Napoleon Hill

Happiness is found in doing - not merely in possessing.
Napoleon Hill

You can't find happiness by robbing another of it. Ditto for economic security.
Napoleon Hill

A smile helps one's looks and makes him feel better without cost.
Napoleon Hill
