Walt Disney

Walt Disney

Walt Disney

Walt Disney

Walt Disney

Walt Disney

Disneyland will never be completed. It will continue to grow as long as there is imagination left in the world.
Walt Disney



Too many people grow up.
Walt Disney

I think it’s important to have a good hard failure when you’re young.
Walt Disney

Walt Disney

Laughter is America’s most important export.
Walt Disney

Adults are only kids grown up…
Walt Disney

When people laugh at Mickey Mouse, it’s because he’s so human.
Walt Disney

I could never convince the financiers that Disneyland was feasible, because dreams offer too little collateral.
Walt Disney

Walt Disney

All our dreams can come true, if we have the courage to pursue them.

Walt Disney

Walt Disney

I am a patient listener, but opinionated to the point of stubbornness when my mind is made up.
Walt Disney

[At an awards speech] Before sitting down to count my blessings, I was to make you a promise. I promise we won’t let this great honor you have paid us tonight go to our heads – we have too many projects for the future to take time out for such a thing. On top of that, after forty-some-odd years of ups and downs in this crazy business of ours, we know too well – you are only as good as your next picture.
Walt Disney

Walt Disney

Walt Disney

Walt Disney
