[On purchasing Viva] Viva was a very significant deal. I could hardly breathe for the whole period we went through the plan because so much was up in the air through that time, but it worked. Moreover, if it worked the first time, we knew we could make it work every time. We leapfrogged our rivals with a big extension in the chain, we removed a key rival brand, and we made money out of the whole thing. It was very good in the end, even if it was bloody tough at the time.
Janine Allis

I read about older entrepreneurs who are billionaires or whatever, and people always ask what keeps them going – don’t they have lots of money? Now I know what keeps them going: it’s the kick they get out of doing deals. Deal-making is like a drug: once you taste it, you want more.
Janine Allis

[On negotiating] You have to be prepared to walk away. If you really feel like that when you are negotiating a deal, you will do a very good deal.
Janine Allis

I want Boost Juice to grow in the best possible manner, I want it to be everywhere, and I want to drive and control that growth every step of the way.
Janine Allis

I’m the engine room making it all happen.
Janine Allis

I think one of the main reasons for our successes is our ability to recognise good people with no egos.
Janine Allis

Arrogance leads to failure and we don’t believe our own bullsh*t.
Janine Allis

If you haven’t got a map or a plan, well, how are you going to get there?… You don’t go on a road trip without a map – life’s just like a road trip.
Janine Allis

The only time I had ever come across the woman thing was after I had done everything.
Janine Allis

I haven’t got this huge ego and think that I know everything… I am a voracious reader.
Janine Allis



I haven’t got any degrees but I am totally tenacious.
Janine Allis

If you do something for the right reasons, everything else will come, including the cash and the success.
Janine Allis

People are what make a business work.
Janine Allis

Pretence gets in the way of success.
Janine Allis

I will, I will, I will, I will – you need all the will in the world to get where you want to go.
Janine Allis

Self-doubt is your greatest enemy.
Janine Allis

Passion is like a drug.
Janine Allis

The person with the answers is you!
Janine Allis

A grudge builds negative energy. As Jeff [Allis] always says, ‘Build a bridge and get over it.’
