A confident person who doesn’t feel the need to explain themselves can create a powerful reaction.
Deborah Meaden

If you are naturally confident it can be easier to make things happen.
Deborah Meaden

A lack of confidence is one of the key reasons for scores of ideas never making it off the ground.
Deborah Meaden

It is generally much easier to do nothing about a good idea than it is to try something and fail.
Deborah Meaden

Everybody has at least a few great ideas…
Deborah Meaden

Everything can be done. Unless it is actually against the laws of physics…
Deborah Meaden

It should be possible to find a route around something to get to a chosen point.
Deborah Meaden

Usually when I hear the worlds ‘I cannot do that’, they are followed by the swift and sharp riposte, ‘So find me someone who can.’
Deborah Meaden

It takes a lot of time and energy to be an entrepreneur.
Deborah Meaden

Entrepreneurs are prepared to take big risks, but we have calculated the outcome against the potential reward and would never dream of leaving it all to luck.
Deborah Meaden



As long as the plan is well thought through, entrepreneurs should be prepared to take a risk. If they are not 100 percent convinced that their business is going to work, why are they even bothering?
Deborah Meaden

If you’re in business, whether at the low end or the top end of the market, just be the best…
Deborah Meaden

I get a high out of seeing a business that I’m involved in flourish.
Deborah Meaden

I’m absolutely useless in the kitchen, in fact I don’t think I’ve cooked a proper meal in 23 years!
Deborah Meaden

[On three words to describe herself.] Frank, Loyal, Insightful.
Deborah Meaden

Sales and marketing isn’t hard or mysterious, you just need to think who is my customer, what do they want, where do I find them and how do I talk to them?
Deborah Meaden

At times I know I can be too blunt.
Deborah Meaden

I can’t stop thinking of ways to improve the businesses I come into contact with!
Deborah Meaden

I’m not a dog person.
Deborah Meaden

Children are very dependent, which is probably why we never had them.
Deborah Meaden



I like being challenged.
Deborah Meaden

You’ve got five people sitting in front of you, who… can take your business from zero to hero like that.
Deborah Meaden

Don’t waste the opportunity.
Deborah Meaden

Marriage to me felt like a commitment and I never really wanted children…
Deborah Meaden

Getting married is the best thing I ever did.
Deborah Meaden

We would have liked children, but we probably didn’t try hard enough. We kept on trying until we realised I was past it…
Deborah Meaden

I met my biological father when I was 16 and felt nothing for him, which is probably a testimony to my stepfather.
Deborah Meaden

I consider it a failure to slog on with a business that is going to die sooner rather than later.
Deborah Meaden

It’s very difficult to realise that you’re not going to make any money out of something. A lot of people are blinded. They think, ‘I can’t give this up. I’d feel like a failure.’
Deborah Meaden

If I hadn’t been very good, I’d have been fired.
Deborah Meaden



I love investing. I love making a difference to businesses.
Deborah Meaden

[In 2008] There’s another substantial business in me; I just need to find it.
Deborah Meaden

When I concentrate, my face is deadpan and I can see there is a coldness there…
Deborah Meaden

When I’m making business decisions I know I can be quite dispassionate.
Deborah Meaden

I don’t suffer fools gladly…
Deborah Meaden

[In 2006] I hope I am softer than comes across…
Deborah Meaden

One of my faults is that I am too blunt at times.
Deborah Meaden

I don’t think of television, I think of the investment.
Deborah Meaden

How did I get in the door?… I just did. I didn’t know you couldn’t.
Deborah Meaden

Deborah Meaden
