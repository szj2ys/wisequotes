Western centric investors often overlook the shift in the global economic axis where emerging industrialised countries now account for about half of global output.
Kerr Neilson

The natural selection process continues to provide winning industries and companies which grow and displace others.
Kerr Neilson

Crises are not as rare as you may think.
Kerr Neilson

You need to have realistic expectations from your share investments.
Kerr Neilson

[In November 2012] The system eventually sorts itself out and after five or ten years, the game will be ‘on’ again.
Kerr Neilson

Dividends play a very important part in rewarding shareholders for the risks they take.
Kerr Neilson

Kerr Neilson

The behaviour of the crowds invariably results in imperfect pricing.

There is a natural predisposition towards extrapolating the present situation far into the future.
