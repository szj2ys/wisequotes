There’s this great quote…which says ‘The future has already arrived. It’s just not evenly distributed yet.’
Jack Dorsey

Our job as founders as entrepreneurs is to distribute the future that’s already here.
Jack Dorsey

Disruption is like an earthquake. Disruption has no purpose. It has not values. It has no organizing principle. It has no direction and it has no leadership… This is not what we want to bring into the world. What we want to bring into the world is revolution.
Jack Dorsey

Revolution has values, revolution has purpose. Revolution has direction. Revolution has leaders. Revolution looks at the intersection ahead and pushes people to do the right thing. And it doesn’t always have to be loud. It doesn’t always have to be violent. It’s just as powerful in it’s stillness.
Jack Dorsey

Speeches will not distribute the future.
Jack Dorsey

We don’t want disruption where we just move things around from point a to point b. We want a direction, we want a purpose.
Jack Dorsey

It’s not going to happen alone.
Jack Dorsey

You don’t have to start from scratch to do something interesting. You don’t have to start from scratch to have a massive impact on the world. You have to have a good idea. You have to convince other people of those good ideas. And you have to push as quickly as possible.
Jack Dorsey

Pick a movement. Pick a revolution. And join it.
Jack Dorsey

Pick a movement. Pick something you believe in. Pick something you want to have an impact in and then question every single thing and be a founder, and be an entrepreneur inside those organizations and inside that movement.
Jack Dorsey



Use technology to remind people that we are really human and civilization is meant to connect together. Civilization is meant to be easy, and the technology to completely disappear. And should be easy to pick up.
Jack Dorsey

[In February 2011] We’ve not done a lot of marketing whatsoever. A lot of it has just been word of mouth.
Jack Dorsey

[In February 2011 on Square.] Starting a startup after twitter is so much easier than before.
Jack Dorsey

We’re trying to identify the key influencers and make them distribution points.
Jack Dorsey

Expect the unexpected. And whenever possible, be the unexpected. I try to live by that on a daily basis.
Jack Dorsey

Don’t take anyone for granted.
Jack Dorsey

Fail openly.
Jack Dorsey

Be humble.
Jack Dorsey

Know when to stop.
Jack Dorsey

I’ve always been fascinated by cities and how they work.
Jack Dorsey



Let’s simplify this entire world.
Jack Dorsey
