Mark Bouris

Mark Bouris

Mark Bouris

But you can negate or neutralise it just by you can creating good things around you by doing good deeds and thinking good thoughts.
Mark Bouris

Mark Bouris

Mark Bouris

Mark Bouris

All borrowers are now part of the capital markets, but benefiting from this new system depends on your understanding of it.
Mark Bouris

Lenders need you to borrow their money.
Mark Bouris

The definition of wealth is what you have minus what you spend and owe.
Mark Bouris

You must know your total cost of funds, not just the interest rate.
Mark Bouris

Never finance long-term investment with short-term facilities.
Mark Bouris

Realistically, the only way you make quick returns in property is through luck or in a boom.
Mark Bouris

Mark Bouris



Don’t accept myths you hear at the pub.
Mark Bouris

Mark Bouris

Mark Bouris

Assets minus liabilities = equity.
Mark Bouris

Mark Bouris
