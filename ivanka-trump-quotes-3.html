The learning curve is steep enough without trying to slog up the hill unassisted…
Ivanka Trump

The worst thing you can do when you’re not sure of a certain path or approach is to fly blind and hope everything will work out…
Ivanka Trump

When I was away at boarding school, my father used to cut out articles from the real estate section of The New York Observer and send them to me with little notes attached. ‘Why do you think?’ he’d write. Or ‘Interesting article.’
Ivanka Trump

Change is a wonderful and necessary aspect of business, but… sometimes things stay the same for a reason.
Ivanka Trump

Be willing to slog through all the stuff at the bottom… if you want to make it to the top.
Ivanka Trump

My parents were always big believers in taking a chance on someone at work, because they knew they themselves wouldn’t have gotten where they were if someone hadn’t taken a chance on them. I try to do the same.
Ivanka Trump

I’m always looking for an edge…
Ivanka Trump

Turn your office into a haven of opportunity, and it can become a kind of magnet, a place where you’re drawn to feel good about yourself and everything you might accomplish.
Ivanka Trump

Showing up is half the battle.
Ivanka Trump

For years, I prided myself on my ability to walk and chew gum at the same time – in a business sense, of course.
Ivanka Trump



If you’re not comfortable saying something in a conference room, in front of your boss, or even in a roomful of strangers, don’t write it in an e-mail…
Ivanka Trump

We haven’t succeeded in redirecting our business because we’ve taken a contrarian view; we’ve succeeded because we’re nimble enough to respond to market conditions.
Ivanka Trump

Be prepared to reinvent yourself, your business, your approach…
Ivanka Trump

The roadblocks that once seemed like speed bumps might now appear insurmountable – but be assured, there’s a way around them.
Ivanka Trump

There’s no fixed road map we’re all meant to follow.
Ivanka Trump

I said, ‘You’ll never guess who I just met with.’ And then I couldn’t wait for him to guess, so I told him. ‘Wow,’ he [Donald Trump] said. ‘You got an audience with Carlos Slim? I guess you really are my daughter!’
Ivanka Trump

The better you know your customers and their needs, the more easily you can sell to them.
Ivanka Trump

[On the jewelry business] Consider these numbers: 90 percent of our decision makers are women; 50 percent buy for themselves; and 40 percent make the selection and simply direct their husbands to our store to close the actual purchase.
Ivanka Trump

Trendy is a dangerous label for a luxury jewelry line, because you want the pieces to endure the test of time.
Ivanka Trump

I often think the best approach to life and career is to reach for everything that makes sense and hold on to what works.
Ivanka Trump



The opportunities are there and all you have to do is get past a few hurdles to grab them…
Ivanka Trump
