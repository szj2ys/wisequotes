103 Amancio Ortega Gaona Quotes

This increase in points of sales which represented approximately two million square meters of retail area as at 31st of January 2008, is the outcome of our strategic expansion lines which have been at the core of our business throughout the latest years: consolidation of our multi-format presence in the European markets and steady growth of our presence in the Asia-Pacific area.
Amancio Ortega Gaona

[In 2008] I believe… I have always made clear to you both my passion for this sector of fashion and my confidence in the specific model in which Inditex is participating, reacting instantly to what the customer is looking for.
Amancio Ortega Gaona

[In 2008] It is this human factor – the motivation of almost 90,000 individuals working towards a single objective – that has allowed Inditex to continue to make a commitment to growth, obtaining outstanding returns from all our investments.
Amancio Ortega Gaona

[In 2008] Our medium and long term strategy has not only not been modified but indeed the fundamental lines set down over the last few years have become even more evident in the course of 2008: we have reinforced the presence of all our concepts on the main European markets, with special attention to those in which we can see the greatest potential for the future, and we have redoubled our commitment to expansion in Asia.
Amancio Ortega Gaona

The commitment made by the company to offer quality from all points of view: design, quality, customer service.
Amancio Ortega Gaona

[In 2008] We are going to continue to deal with our business challenges, our strategic and social commitments, from the positions that have always characterised the performance of this company; the deep in involvement of our teams in the project thanks to an intense and shared corporate culture; the constant effort to innovate; the search for efficiency in all facets of our activity; and the certainty of sharing values that bind our company with the society in which we live.
Amancio Ortega Gaona

[In 2009] All employees, with the latest fashion trends foremost in mind, perform their jobs while paying close attention to environmental and social issues, which confers a high degree of responsibility to their creative work.
Amancio Ortega Gaona

By the end of 2009 the Group consisted of 92,302 individuals of more than 140 nationalities, who spoke 40 different languages, and resided in more than 80 countries. This diversity of cultures and career paths is one of Inditex’s greatest assets.
Amancio Ortega Gaona

[On having 140 nationalities working in Inditex and having such a diverse workforce] It translates into an incomparable exchange and richness of cultures.
Amancio Ortega Gaona

[On employee diversity] Such diversity is one of Inditex’s best resources for gaining first-hand knowledge of international trends and creating the best-possible fashion collections to keep up with the hottest trends and changing tastes of shoppers worldwide.
Amancio Ortega Gaona



Dynamic decision-making, common sense, a sense of commitment and self-criticism guided by motivating leaders are the key values underpinning this shared determination to take fashion to all corners of the globe.
Amancio Ortega Gaona

Store employees have the best understanding of both customer tastes and their stores’ energy consumption, and of where sustainability improvements should be applied.
Amancio Ortega Gaona

[In 2009] We are convinced that our supply chain requirements, which are always complemented by monitoring and advisory programmes to encourage improvements in working conditions at our suppliers’ factories, represent significant progress for the entire textile industry.
Amancio Ortega Gaona

The primary goal of satisfying the appetite for fashion of our numerous customers worldwide. This requires a constant quest for innovation, creativity and cultural involvement at all international levels.
Amancio Ortega Gaona

We were facing with confidence the test of putting our capacity for profitable growth in the long term to the scrutiny of investors. That confidence was based on the trust that arises from the shared effort in a project in which all those taking part believed and continue to believe with absolute sincerity.
Amancio Ortega Gaona

[In 2010] Our Group is a company with a global footprint present in almost eighty countries and which has eight different chains.
Amancio Ortega Gaona

[In 2010] The demands which the economic environment has made of us have made clear the strength of this business model, its capacity to adapt in a flexible manner to the demands of the market, and the solidity of its expectations of profitable growth in the future.
Amancio Ortega Gaona

2010 has been a significant year both from the point of view of the economic results and of commercial achievements. It is a year in which Inditex has achieved 100,000 employees and has continued to create stable employment, both in its stores and at its logistical centres and corporate headquarters.
Amancio Ortega Gaona

[2010] The customer must continue to be our main centre of attention, both in the creation of our fashion collections and in the design of our shops, of our logistical system and of any other activity.
Amancio Ortega Gaona

We have continued to advance in the application of two of the main axes of our strategy, rooted in the sensitivity both of the organization and of the environment: the environment and social responsibility.
Amancio Ortega Gaona



Australia has been added in the first few months of 2011 and South Africa will do the same before the end of the year.
Amancio Ortega Gaona

In 2010, we also began the online sale of Zara products in sixteen European countries…
Amancio Ortega Gaona

[In 2009] Inditex is undoubtedly the world’s most beautiful company.
Amancio Ortega Gaona
