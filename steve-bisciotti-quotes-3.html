Good fortune. Just good fortune.
Steve Bisciotti

[In January 2013] So my expectations are for us to win with class, and if you lose then maintain your class and your dignity.
Steve Bisciotti

Hold on and hear me out, cause I’m going to sound like a typical fan.
Steve Bisciotti

It’s my opinion, just because I share it with a million others doesn’t mean that I don’t deserve to have that opinion. Walk me through it, explain why I’m wrong.
Steve Bisciotti

That’s me being the superfan. I get to talk with John and Ozzie.
Steve Bisciotti

It’s always something.
Steve Bisciotti

Even though I feel like a fan and have the same concerns that they do…
Steve Bisciotti

I get my answer a lot quicker than they do, so I don’t worry about it as long as the fans do.
Steve Bisciotti

[On why he bought the Baltimore Ravens.] Because I’m a fan. But now that I own the team I can walk down the hall and ask the head coach and Ozzie - ‘Why are we doing this?’
Steve Bisciotti

The playoffs is a roll.
Steve Bisciotti



Things happen.
Steve Bisciotti

What are denials any more? People believe what they want to believe.
Steve Bisciotti

I’ve got to believe that Ozzie does a little bit more prep than me.
Steve Bisciotti

We knew early on we had picked the right guy to be a partner… The buck stops with John on so many decisions.
Steve Bisciotti

[In January 2013] The economists will tell you know that [using] state money helping build stadiums and things like that is a waste… It’s not a waste.
Steve Bisciotti

We see the disappointment in people when we lose.
Steve Bisciotti

It’s a value that you can’t put a dollar sign on it. The pride thing.
Steve Bisciotti

[On holding up a trophy one hand.] I’ve held it before and I’m ready to do it again.
Steve Bisciotti

We hate night games, we always do. It’s a long day.
Steve Bisciotti

[On his time with Art Modell] It was just special.
Steve Bisciotti



[On Art Modell] I’m going to remember the smile more than anything.
Steve Bisciotti

I never lost faith in the team.
Steve Bisciotti
