By thinking and acting affirmatively in this minute, you will influence the hour, the day, and in time, your entire life.
Denis Waitley

Denis Waitley

Your attitude is either the lock on or key to your door of success.
Denis Waitley

Denis Waitley

A smile is the light in your window that lets others know there is a caring person inside.
Denis Waitley

The most important three words you can say to yourself: “Yes, I can!”
Denis Waitley

If you believe you can… you probably can. If you believer you won’t…you most assuredly won’t.
Denis Waitley

Belief is the ignition switch that gets you off the launching pad.
Denis Waitley

We learn by observation, imitation and repetition.
Denis Waitley

Habits begin as offhanded remarks, ideas and igages. And then, layer upon layer, through practice, they grow from cobwebs into cables that shackle or strengthen our lives.
Denis Waitley



Habits are like submarines. They run silent and deep.
Denis Waitley

First we make our habits, then our habits make us.
Denis Waitley

Habits are like comfortable beds. They are easy to get into, but difficult to get out of.
Denis Waitley

Winning and losing are both learned habits.
Denis Waitley

I’d rather watch a winner, then hear one any day;
Denis Waitley

So please, my loving parents let your lives show me the way.
Denis Waitley

I’m only a reflection of what you taught today I may misunderstand you and the high advice you give;
Denis Waitley

But there’s no misunderstanding how you act and how you live.
Denis Waitley

So teach me by example, don’t preach about what’s right;
Denis Waitley

And show me by your actions every day and night.
Denis Waitley



I know that you’re not perfect, in the things you do and say;
Denis Waitley

And the lectures you deliver are to help me find my way;
Denis Waitley

If you have real, internal value, you don’t need a loud, expensive imitation.
Denis Waitley

It is not what you get that makes you successful, it is what you are continuing to do with what you’ve got.
Denis Waitley

Identify with excellence, put your name on your work, and both your work and name will stand the test of time.
Denis Waitley

It is not so much what the job gives you, it’s what you give to the job.
Denis Waitley

Give your best effort, because you are worth your best effort.
Denis Waitley

Denis Waitley

Expect the best, plan for the worst, and prepare to be surprised.
Denis Waitley

Denis Waitley

Denis Waitley
