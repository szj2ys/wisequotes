George Washington Carver

George Washington Carver

George Washington Carver

George Washington Carver

I come from Tuskegee, Alabama. I am engaged in agricultural research work, and I have given some attention to the peanut, but not as much as I expect to give.
George Washington Carver

George Washington Carver

George Washington Carver

George Washington Carver

George Washington Carver

George Washington Carver

George Washington Carver

George Washington Carver

George Washington Carver

George Washington Carver

George Washington Carver

George Washington Carver

George Washington Carver

George Washington Carver

George Washington Carver

George Washington Carver

George Washington Carver

George Washington Carver

George Washington Carver

George Washington Carver

George Washington Carver
