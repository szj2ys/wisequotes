At some point in a business cycle one has to get greedy. And the time to get greedy is when everybody’s running for the hills with fear.
Bruce Berkowitz

We are running toward that which most people are running away from.
Bruce Berkowitz

There are two ways to invest: you can predict or you can react.
Bruce Berkowitz

Ignore the crowd.
Bruce Berkowitz

My definition of skill is knowing when you’re lucky and taking advantage of that luck.
Bruce Berkowitz

What I try to do is focus on the facts of today.
Bruce Berkowitz

What worries me is knowing that it is usually a person’s last investment idea that kills them.
Bruce Berkowitz

I don’t invest in things I can’t understand.
Bruce Berkowitz

By ignoring the crowd, we find opportunities in stocks that people are running away from.
Bruce Berkowitz

Our biggest mistakes have always involved overestimating management.
Bruce Berkowitz



Cash is the equivalent of financial Valium. It keeps you cool, calm and collected.
Bruce Berkowitz

At then end of the day, cash is the only thing you can spend.
Bruce Berkowitz

Cash is really valuable when no one has it.
Bruce Berkowitz

[In December 2007] You need only a few good ideas in a lifetime to become wealthier than you ever thought possible.
Bruce Berkowitz

Investing is all about comparing what you give versus what you are going to get over time.
Bruce Berkowitz

A lot of people discovered that leverage is a two edged sword.
Bruce Berkowitz

We tend to react rather than predict.
Bruce Berkowitz

The negatives are all uncertainty about the future. What I try to do is to focus on the facts of today.
Bruce Berkowitz

[September 2011] The banks have the strongest balances sheets they’ve had probably in their histories.
Bruce Berkowitz

Our brains are wired for over-reaction and momentum.
Bruce Berkowitz



Count what matters. So we count the cash.
Bruce Berkowitz

[On US banks lending policies in September 2011] Everyone comments on how tough it is to get a loan. Because they’ve gone from no documentation to unbelievable documentation. But it’s the nature…
Bruce Berkowitz

You don’t want to be in denial. So you take out your checklist with the 500 aspects that you look at…
Bruce Berkowitz

You have to have the courage of your convictions. That’s what you are getting paid for. This is the time when I really earn my money.
Bruce Berkowitz

The worst situation is you’re backed into a corner and you can’t get out of it.
Bruce Berkowitz

[In 2010] You don’t have the money to buy more or you don’t have the flexibility, that’s a nightmare scenario.
Bruce Berkowitz

[In 2010] Great investors never run out of cash. We always want to have a lot of cash.
Bruce Berkowitz

It depends on… where you believe you are in the cycle. If you think that you’re bouncing around the bottom and you’ve already paid the price for having the courage of your convictions, then I don’t think that we need the cash around that we do.
Bruce Berkowitz

If I’m wrong then I don’t deserve to be in business.
Bruce Berkowitz

[September 2011] There’s a fear of the future but I don’t understand the math that’s being applied to the forecast of the future. I do know that fear is being reflected in the price that you’re paying for Bank of America…
Bruce Berkowitz



[February 2000] Concentrated investing implies less risk of permanent loss as long as you maintain superior knowledge about the companies you own.
Bruce Berkowitz

[In 2011] AIG will not be paying taxes for many years given it’s previous losses.
Bruce Berkowitz

Is there a sufficient margin of safety…
Bruce Berkowitz

[In 2010] As you get bigger you put more into your investments and that last idea, which may be bad, will end up losing more than you’ve made over a decade.
Bruce Berkowitz

[In 2010] We’re two thirds invested in equities today, not a kamikaze strategy.
Bruce Berkowitz

It’s just a question of time.
Bruce Berkowitz

You can’t be 100% positive about anything. To have a fanatical belief would be a mistake.
Bruce Berkowitz

No one wants to through out thirty years of hard work.
Bruce Berkowitz

[September 2011] It’s been six months, in February I was a hero and now I’m a bum so we’ll see six months from now. Revenge should be sweet.
Bruce Berkowitz

If I don’t understand something, I’ve learned to walk away.
Bruce Berkowitz
