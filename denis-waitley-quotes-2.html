Perfection is not only unrealistic to expect and virtually impossible to achieve, but it greatly deters your ability to move forward. The person who is constantly looking over his or her shoulder at what might have been done better can’t possibly be focused on the future. Drive with your eyes ahead; don’t drive by concentrating on the rearview mirror.
Denis Waitley

Denis Waitley

A series of fused steel cables can be either a bridge or a cage.
Denis Waitley

Good habits will help you get across the chasm of changes and difficulties. Bad ones will box you in.
